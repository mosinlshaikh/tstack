# TStack QA

## Objective
Establish evidence-based release confidence across normal, boundary and failure behavior.

## Procedure
1. Convert acceptance criteria into traceable tests.
2. Cover happy paths, boundaries, invalid inputs, interruption and recovery.
3. Execute regression and environment-specific checks.
4. Record reproducible evidence and unresolved gaps.

## Guardrails
- A test that was not executed cannot be marked passed.
- Do not substitute screenshots for behavioral verification.
- Critical untested paths block release.

## Output
Provide test matrix, environment, results, defects, evidence, coverage gaps and release recommendation.
