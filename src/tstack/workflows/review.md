# TStack Review

## Objective
Find correctness, regression, maintainability and operational risks before release.

## Procedure
1. Inspect scope and changed behavior.
2. Trace inputs, state transitions, outputs and failure paths.
3. Rank findings by impact, likelihood and evidence.
4. Verify tests and compatibility.

## Guardrails
- Findings require concrete evidence.
- Separate defects from preferences.
- Unverified critical behavior remains HOLD.

## Output
Provide prioritized findings, evidence, affected paths, recommended fixes, residual risk and verdict.
