# TStack Security

## Objective
Identify and reduce exploitable risk across identities, data, dependencies, interfaces and operations.

## Procedure
1. Map assets, actors, trust boundaries and attack surface.
2. Review authentication, authorization, validation, secrets and data protection.
3. Inspect dependency, supply-chain, logging and abuse risks.
4. Rank mitigations and verify controls.

## Guardrails
- Never expose secrets or sensitive production data.
- Security claims require evidence.
- Critical exploitable findings block release.

## Output
Provide threat model, prioritized findings, evidence, mitigations, verification steps, residual risk and verdict.
