# Donations and Sponsorship

TStack is developed by T Technology Research Lab. Donations help fund continued
development, testing, documentation, security hardening, and future local-first
agentic engineering features.

## Sponsor

- GitHub Sponsors: `mosinlshaikh`

## Important Terms

Donations and sponsorships are voluntary support. They do not grant:

- commercial use rights;
- production use rights;
- redistribution rights;
- private support guarantees;
- warranty;
- trademark rights;
- ownership of the project;
- access to private roadmap items.

Commercial, enterprise, hosted-service, OEM, white-label, government, or
production use requires a separate written license from T Technology Research
Lab.

## Funding Priorities

Sponsorship funds are intended for:

- runtime kernel development;
- security and sandbox hardening;
- release trust and provenance work;
- documentation;
- test coverage;
- local-first agent tooling;
- future Blender, browser, mobile, and creation workflows after the runtime
  safety foundation is mature.
